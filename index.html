About Me

Started my career as a contract laborer in Kuwait Oil Company Wafra. It was a joint operation between Kuwait and Saudi - Khafji. I worked as a maintenance engineer for pumps in the desalination plant and for the main shipping pumps which were shipping crude to Kuwait KNPC in Fahaheel and to GOC Saudia. I was also taking care of electrical maintenance like motors, caterpillar engine's alternators and centralized A/C plants. After surviving the Gulf war, we were called back for the renovation and rebuilding of the desalination plant in Wafra - Kuwait.

After returning back to India, I joined as a senior service engineer in Fluidline Systems & Controls, dealers of KSB pumps in Chennai.

Overall, I specialized in troubleshooting the causes of pump failures. Additionally, I developed skills in analyzing system failures, such as reduced pump discharge, low system head or system pressure, excess power drawn by the pump's driver, pump bearing failure, motor failure due to overheating or excess power, motor bearing failure, impeller failure due to cavitation or corrosion, and mechanical seal failures. I also analyzed and recommended proper seal cooling systems with appropriate cooling plans for mechanical seals.

Career

Fluid Line

During my career in Fluidline, I was taking care of installation and commissioning of pumps like back pullout pumps, vertical and horizontal high-pressure pumps, deep bore well pumps, diaphragm pumps, triplex plunger pumps, and submersible sewage pumps. I installed and commissioned pumps in various industries, including:

  • Automobile industry
  • Water treatment plant R.O plant
  • Sewage treatment plant
  • Paper industry
  • Glass factory
  • Refinery industry
  • Fertilizer industry
  • Cement factory
  • Pharmaceutical industry
  • Firefighting applications (fire hydrant pumps, sprinkler pumps, engine-driven fire pumps)

Startup

Alex Engineering Services

In 2005, I started my own servicing company under the name Alex Engineering Services. In addition to my servicing, I also rendered service support to M/s. Flowtech Engineers, who were the dealers and distributors for Flowserve pumps.

Projects with Flowtech Engineers

While supporting Flowtech Engineers, I was honored by a post as service manager for all types of service activities. Some of the key projects handled include:

  • Installation and commissioning of high-pressure pumps at R.O plant in PDO Oman.
